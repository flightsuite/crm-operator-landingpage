1 Who We Are

FlightSuite Corporation, a Delaware corporation ("FlightSuite," "we," "us," or "our"), is the data controller for the personal information collected through the FlightSuite Chrome extension and website at flightsuite.ai.

FlightSuite is a Chrome browser extension that automates CRM data entry using natural language, currently supporting GoHighLevel and HubSpot.

2 Information We Collect

We collect information in the following categories:

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

Type Examples When Collected
Contact Information Name, email address Early access sign-up, contact forms
Account Credentials Authentication tokens for connected CRM platforms When you connect a CRM account
Natural Language Commands Text instructions you type to update your CRM During active use of the extension
Feedback & Support Messages, bug reports, feature requests When you contact our support

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

Type Examples
Usage Data Feature interactions, command frequency, error events
Technical Data Browser version, extension version, operating system
Log Data IP address, timestamps, pages visited on flightsuite.ai
Analytics Aggregated usage statistics via privacy-respecting analytics tools

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect, store, or transmit your CRM contacts' personal data to our servers.
  • We do not record keystrokes beyond the natural language commands you explicitly submit.
  • We do not access browsing history on pages unrelated to supported CRM platforms.
  • We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

4 How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information in the following limited circumstances:

Service Providers

We engage trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating the Service. These providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and may not use it for their own purposes.

  • AI Model Providers: Natural language commands may be processed by AI model APIs (e.g., Anthropic Claude) to interpret your intent. Only the command text is transmitted — no CRM records or personal contact data.
  • Analytics: Aggregated, anonymized usage data for product improvement.
  • Cloud Infrastructure: Hosting and storage providers.
  • Customer Support: Tools used to manage support requests.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request, or if we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect rights, property, or safety.

Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, financing, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of the transaction. We will notify you before your information becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

With Your Consent

We may share information with third parties when you have given us your explicit consent to do so.

5 CRM Data & Third-Party Platforms

FlightSuite operates within your browser session and interacts directly with your connected CRM platform (e.g., GoHighLevel, HubSpot) using your existing authenticated session or API credentials.

CRM actions (creating contacts, updating deals, logging calls, etc.) are executed directly between the extension and the CRM platform's API. FlightSuite does not create a separate copy of your CRM data on our servers.

Your use of connected CRM platforms is governed by those platforms' own terms of service and privacy policies. We encourage you to review:

  • GoHighLevel: Privacy Policy
  • HubSpot: Privacy Policy

6 Cookies & Tracking

Website (flightsuite.ai)

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies for the following purposes:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the website to function properly.
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand how visitors use the site (aggregated, anonymized).
  • Preference cookies: Remember your settings and choices.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect your experience on our website.

Chrome Extension

The FlightSuite Chrome extension does not use advertising cookies or cross-site tracking. It accesses browser storage only to maintain your settings and session state.

7 Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide the Service and fulfill the purposes described in this Policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

  • Account and contact information: Retained while your account is active and for up to 2 years after account closure for legal and compliance purposes.
  • Usage and log data: Typically retained for 12 months.
  • Support communications: Retained for 3 years after resolution.
  • Natural language command logs: Retained for up to 90 days for debugging purposes, then deleted or anonymized.

You may request deletion of your personal information at any time (see Your Rights below).

8 Security

We implement industry-standard technical and organizational security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit (TLS/HTTPS)
  • Encryption of sensitive data at rest
  • Access controls and authentication requirements for internal systems
  • Regular security reviews of our infrastructure

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security. If you believe your security has been compromised, please contact us immediately.

10 Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If you are a parent or guardian and believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 18, please contact us immediately and we will take steps to delete the information.

For users in the EEA or UK, we rely on appropriate safeguards for international transfers, such as Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the European Commission, where required.
