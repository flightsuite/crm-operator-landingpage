How We Calculate

Our calculator uses industry-validated benchmarks and research-backed assumptions:

  • 5.5 hours/week: Average time sales reps spend on CRM data entry (Salesforce, HubSpot research)
  • 52 weeks: Standard work year
  • 85% reduction: Conservative estimate based on FlightSuite user data (actual range: 80-95%)
  • 8-hour days: Standard calculation for converting hours to "selling days"

These calculations don't include secondary benefits like improved data quality, better forecasting accuracy, or increased CRM adoption—all of which further improve ROI.
