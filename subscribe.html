Questions

Is the Chrome extension really free forever?

Yes. The Chrome extension — which lets you update your CRM by typing naturally in your browser — will always be free with no usage limits. The subscription only applies to SMS and WhatsApp AI queries.

What counts as an SMS/WA credit?

Every time you send a text or WhatsApp message to FlightSuite and it processes your request, that uses one credit. Reading replies or confirmations doesn't count.

What happens when I run out of credits?

Your Chrome extension keeps working normally — unlimited. SMS/WA queries will get a friendly reply asking you to upgrade. Your CRM data is never affected.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel at any time from your account portal. You keep your credits until the end of the billing period. No questions asked.

I'm already subscribed — how do I manage my plan?

Sign in and click "Manage plan" to access the Stripe Customer Portal where you can upgrade, downgrade, update payment, or cancel.
