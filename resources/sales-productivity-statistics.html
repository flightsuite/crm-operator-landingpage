50 Sales Productivity Statistics for 2026

Last updated: January 2026 All sources cited

Sales productivity is under more pressure than ever. Reps face increasing administrative burdens while quotas keep rising. We've compiled 50 essential statistics—all with original sources—to help you understand the current state of sales productivity and make the case for automation.

Use these statistics in your presentations, business cases, and reports. All data comes from reputable research organizations and can be cited with the sources provided.

5.5 hrs

Source: HubSpot Sales Statistics
21%

Source: CSO Insights Sales Performance Study
440 hrs

Source: CSO Insights
23%

Key Takeaway

Sales reps spend only one-third of their time actually selling. Administrative tasks—especially CRM data entry—consume the majority of their workday. Eliminating 5.5 hours per week of data entry would increase selling time by 40%.
